# MMA Squared

**The home of MMA fight breakdowns and data-driven analytics**

---

## About Us

MMA Squared is created by Andy and Abdul (the A that get's squared in MMA^2). We've been friends since high school and have been wrestling all our lives. We're martial arists, students of the game, and MMA nerds. We hope you enjoy our insights and analytics. 

---

## What We Do

We combine our deep expertise in combat sports with factual data. Abdul is a software engineer and data scientist, he leverages his experience to gather data, and create useful visuals. Andy is a harcore analyst who can breakdown fights into comprehensible pieces. Together, we combine our love for data-driven analytics with our deep passion for the sport to create thoughtful breakdowns. 

We hope you like your analysis, you can find our content below.
